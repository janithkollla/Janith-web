How to Increase YouTube Views

  • Optimize Thumbnails: Use bright, eye-catching images with bold text.
  • Engaging Titles: Write clear, keyword-rich titles to attract clicks.
  • Consistent Posting: Upload videos regularly to keep your audience engaged.
  • Promote on Social Media: Share videos on platforms like WhatsApp and Instagram.
  • Engage with Comments: Respond to viewers to build a community.

YouTube History

  • 2005: YouTube founded by Steve Chen, Chad Hurley, and Jawed Karim.
  • 2006: Acquired by Google for $1.65 billion.
  • 2010: Introduced 4K video support.
  • 2015: Launched YouTube Premium and YouTube Kids.
  • 2020: Shorts introduced to compete with TikTok.
